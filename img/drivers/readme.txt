put drivers pictures in this folder
steamid.png|.jpg

look for
info_picture_width = 300
info_picture_height = 300
in the config.ini to change the ratio (not in the config widget)
300 is the number of pixels at 1080p.
1440p, 4k or any other resolution will be scaled from this number
ex: 600px at 4k = 300px at 1080p
